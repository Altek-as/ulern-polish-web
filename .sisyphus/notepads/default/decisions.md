## CSS Design Decisions for uLern-Polish VR
- **Aesthetic**: Futuristic Polish theme with deep space/night sky background and vibrant neon red/white accents (Polish flag colors).
- **Typography**: Poppins for headings (bold, modern), Roboto for body text (clean, readable).
- **Layout**: Full-screen flexbox overlay with a glassmorphism card for content.
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first approach with specific media queries for small screens (max-width: 480px) and landscape orientation (max-height: 500px) to accommodate VR headset insertion.
- **Interactivity**: Hover and active states on the start button with a glowing pulse effect on the logo to draw attention.
